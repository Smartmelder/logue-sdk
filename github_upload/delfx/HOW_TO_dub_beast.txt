================================================================================
DUB BEAST - ULTIMATE RHYTHMIC DELAY
================================================================================

FEATURES:
- 6 delay modes (Groove, Dub, Burst, Reverse, Shimmer, Ping-Pong)
- 16 tempo divisions (1/4 to 1/64, dotted, triplets)
- Saturated feedback with color filter
- Bit crusher for lo-fi repeats
- Stereo spread with cross-feedback
- Ducking (sidechain compression)
- Pitch shift in feedback (shimmer)
- Modulation (chorus-like movement)
- Freeze mode (infinite hold)
- Auto-pan delays

================================================================================
HARDWARE CONTROLS
================================================================================

TYPE KNOB (Knob A):
  → Controls MODE (delay type)
  → 0: Groove, 1: Dub, 2: Burst, 3: Reverse, 4: Shimmer, 5: Ping-Pong

SHAPE KNOB (Knob B):
  → Controls TIME (tempo division)
  → 0-15: Various tempo divisions

SHIFT + B (MIX):
  → Controls DRY/WET MIX
  → -100% = full dry, 0% = balanced, +100% = full wet

================================================================================
11 PARAMETERS
================================================================================

0. MODE (TYPE knob)
   Range: 0-5
   Default: 1 (Dub)
   Use: Delay mode selection
   - 0: Groove (short, tight, ducked)
   - 1: Dub (long, dark, saturated)
   - 2: Burst (very short, high feedback)
   - 3: Reverse (backwards delay tails)
   - 4: Shimmer (pitch-shifted feedback)
   - 5: Ping-Pong (L/R bounce)

1. TIME (SHAPE knob)
   Range: 0-15
   Default: 4 (1/8 dotted)
   Use: Tempo division
   - 0-2: 1/4 (straight, dotted, triplet)
   - 3-5: 1/8 (straight, dotted, triplet)
   - 6-8: 1/16 (straight, dotted, triplet)
   - 9-11: 1/32 (straight, dotted, triplet)
   - 12: 1/64 straight
   - 13-15: Polyrhythmic (3/16, 5/16, 7/16)

2. FEEDBACK
   Range: 0-100%
   Default: 60%
   Use: Feedback amount
   - 0%: No repeats
   - 50%: Moderate repeats
   - 100%: Maximum feedback

3. MIX (SHIFT + B)
   Range: -100% to +100%
   Default: 0% (Balanced)
   Use: Dry/wet balance
   - -100%: Full dry
   - 0%: Balanced (50/50)
   - +100%: Full wet

4. COLOR
   Range: 0-100%
   Default: 40%
   Use: Tone filter
   - 0%: Dark (low-pass)
   - 50%: Neutral
   - 100%: Bright (high-pass)

5. GRIT
   Range: 0-100%
   Default: 30%
   Use: Saturation/drive amount
   - 0%: Clean
   - 50%: Moderate saturation
   - 100%: Maximum saturation

6. STEREO
   Range: 0-100%
   Default: 50%
   Use: Stereo spread
   - 0%: Mono
   - 50%: Normal stereo
   - 100%: Ultra-wide

7. DUCKING
   Range: 0-100%
   Default: 30%
   Use: Sidechain compression amount
   - 0%: No ducking
   - 50%: Moderate ducking
   - 100%: Maximum ducking

8. MODULATION
   Range: 0-100%
   Default: 20%
   Use: Modulation depth
   - 0%: No modulation
   - 50%: Moderate modulation
   - 100%: Maximum modulation

9. PITCH
   Range: -12 to +12 semitones
   Default: 0
   Use: Pitch shift in feedback
   - -12: One octave down
   - 0: No pitch shift
   - +12: One octave up

10. FREEZE
    Range: 0-1 (OFF/ON)
    Default: 0 (OFF)
    Use: Infinite hold mode
    - 0: OFF (normal delay)
    - 1: ON (freeze buffer)
