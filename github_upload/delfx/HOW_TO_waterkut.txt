================================================================================
WATERKUT - RAINDROP DELAY EFFECT V2
================================================================================

FEATURES:
- 10 parallel delay lines (raindrop effect)
- Tempo-synced delay times
- Randomization/chaos control
- Modulation (LFO movement)
- Tone filter (dark to bright)
- Stereo width control
- Line count control (1-10 active delays)
- Diffusion (smoothness)
- Freeze mode (infinite hold)

================================================================================
HARDWARE CONTROLS
================================================================================

TYPE KNOB (Knob A):
  → Controls TIME (delay length scale)
  → Higher = longer delays

SHAPE KNOB (Knob B):
  → Controls DEPTH/FEEDBACK
  → Higher = more feedback

SHIFT + B (MIX):
  → Controls DRY/WET MIX
  → -100% = full dry, 0% = balanced, +100% = full wet

================================================================================
11 PARAMETERS
================================================================================

0. TIME (TYPE knob)
   Range: 0-100%
   Default: 80%
   Use: Delay length scale
   - 0%: Short delays (0.1s)
   - 50%: Medium delays (1.5s)
   - 100%: Long delays (3.0s)

1. DEPTH/FEEDBACK (SHAPE knob)
   Range: 0-100%
   Default: 75%
   Use: Feedback amount
   - 0%: No repeats
   - 50%: Moderate repeats
   - 100%: Maximum feedback

2. MIX (SHIFT + B)
   Range: -100% to +100%
   Default: 0% (Balanced)
   Use: Dry/wet balance
   - -100%: Full dry
   - 0%: Balanced (50/50)
   - +100%: Full wet

3. CHAOS
   Range: 0-100%
   Default: 50%
   Use: Randomization amount
   - 0%: No chaos (deterministic)
   - 50%: Moderate chaos
   - 100%: Maximum chaos

4. MOD INTENSITY
   Range: 0-100%
   Default: 30%
   Use: Modulation depth
   - 0%: No modulation
   - 50%: Moderate modulation
   - 100%: Maximum modulation

5. MOD RATE
   Range: 0-100%
   Default: 10% (~1 Hz)
   Use: Modulation speed
   - 0%: Very slow (0.1 Hz)
   - 50%: Moderate (5 Hz)
   - 100%: Fast (10 Hz)

6. TONE
   Range: 0-100%
   Default: 50%
   Use: Tone filter
   - 0%: Dark (low-pass)
   - 50%: Neutral
   - 100%: Bright (high-pass)

7. STEREO WIDTH
   Range: 0-100%
   Default: 75% (150%)
   Use: Stereo width (0-200%)
   - 0%: Mono
   - 50%: Normal stereo (100%)
   - 100%: Ultra-wide (200%)

8. LINES
   Range: 1-10
   Default: 10
   Use: Number of active delay lines
   - 1: Single delay
   - 5: Moderate (5 lines)
   - 10: Maximum (all lines)

9. DIFFUSION
   Range: 0-100%
   Default: 40%
   Use: Smoothness/diffusion
   - 0%: No diffusion (sharp)
   - 50%: Moderate diffusion
   - 100%: Maximum diffusion (smooth)

10. FREEZE
    Range: 0-1 (OFF/ON)
    Default: 0 (OFF)
    Use: Infinite hold mode
    - 0: OFF (normal delay)
    - 1: ON (freeze buffer)
