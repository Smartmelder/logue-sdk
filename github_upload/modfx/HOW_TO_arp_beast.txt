================================================================================
ARP BEAST - ADVANCED ARPEGGIATOR
================================================================================

FEATURES:
- 16 pattern types
- 1-4 octave range
- Swing control
- Gate time control
- Accent system
- Probability (note skipping)
- 8 harmony modes
- 8 tempo multipliers
- Randomization
- Dry/wet mix

================================================================================
HARDWARE CONTROLS
================================================================================

TYPE KNOB (Knob A):
  → Controls PATTERN (0-15)
  → Selects arpeggio pattern

SHAPE KNOB (Knob B):
  → Controls OCTAVES (1-4)
  → Higher = more octaves

================================================================================
10 PARAMETERS
================================================================================

0. PATTERN (TYPE knob)
   Range: 0-15
   Default: varies
   Use: Arpeggio pattern selection
   - 0-15: Different patterns

1. OCTAVES (SHAPE knob)
   Range: 1-4
   Default: varies
   Use: Octave range
   - 1: Single octave
   - 2: Two octaves
   - 3: Three octaves
   - 4: Four octaves

2. SWING
   Range: 0-100%
   Default: varies
   Use: Swing/shuffle amount
   - 0%: Straight timing
   - 50%: Moderate swing
   - 100%: Maximum swing

3. GATE
   Range: 0-100%
   Default: varies
   Use: Gate time (note length)
   - 0%: Very short
   - 50%: Moderate gate
   - 100%: Full gate

4. ACCENT
   Range: 0-100%
   Default: varies
   Use: Accent amount
   - 0%: No accent
   - 50%: Moderate accent
   - 100%: Maximum accent

5. PROBABILITY
   Range: 0-100%
   Default: varies
   Use: Note skip probability
   - 0%: No skipping
   - 50%: Moderate skipping
   - 100%: Maximum skipping

6. HARMONY
   Range: 0-7
   Default: varies
   Use: Harmony mode
   - 0-7: Different harmony modes

7. TEMPO MULT
   Range: 0-7
   Default: varies
   Use: Tempo multiplier
   - 0-7: Different tempo multipliers

8. RANDOMIZE
   Range: 0-100%
   Default: varies
   Use: Randomization amount
   - 0%: No randomization
   - 50%: Moderate randomization
   - 100%: Maximum randomization

9. MIX
   Range: 0-100%
   Default: varies
   Use: Dry/wet balance
   - 0%: Full dry
   - 50%: Balanced
   - 100%: Full wet
