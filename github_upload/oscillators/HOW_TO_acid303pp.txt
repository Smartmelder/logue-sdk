================================================================================
ACID 303++ - ULTIMATE ACID GROOVE MACHINE
================================================================================

FEATURES:
- 4-pole Moog ladder filter
- Pre/Post distortion with flavors
- Dual filter modes (Serial/Parallel/BP/Notch)
- Sub oscillator engine (-1/-2 octave)
- Sample & Hold LFO
- Envelope follower modulation
- Filter keyboard tracking
- Accent dynamics system
- Morphing waveforms
- Slide with auto-overlap detection
- Built-in sequencer

================================================================================
HARDWARE CONTROLS
================================================================================

TYPE KNOB (Knob A):
  → Controls CUTOFF (filter cutoff)
  → Higher = brighter

SHAPE KNOB (Knob B):
  → Controls SLIDE (portamento)
  → Higher = longer slide

================================================================================
10 PARAMETERS
================================================================================

0. CUTOFF (TYPE knob)
   Range: 0-100%
   Default: varies
   Use: Filter cutoff frequency
   - 0%: Closed (dark)
   - 50%: Mid (balanced)
   - 100%: Open (bright)

1. SLIDE (SHAPE knob)
   Range: 0-100%
   Default: varies
   Use: Portamento/slide time
   - 0%: No slide
   - 50%: Moderate slide
   - 100%: Long slide

2. RESONANCE
   Range: 0-100%
   Default: varies
   Use: Filter resonance amount
   - 0%: No resonance
   - 50%: Moderate resonance
   - 100%: Maximum resonance

3. ENV AMOUNT
   Range: 0-100%
   Default: varies
   Use: Envelope modulation amount
   - 0%: No envelope modulation
   - 50%: Moderate modulation
   - 100%: Maximum modulation

4. WAVEFORM
   Range: 0-3
   Default: varies
   Use: Waveform selection
   - 0-3: Different waveforms

5. AMP DECAY
   Range: 0-100%
   Default: varies
   Use: Amplitude envelope decay
   - 0%: Quick decay
   - 50%: Moderate decay
   - 100%: Slow decay

6. FLT DECAY
   Range: 0-100%
   Default: varies
   Use: Filter envelope decay
   - 0%: Quick decay
   - 50%: Moderate decay
   - 100%: Slow decay

7. DISTORTION
   Range: -100% to +100%
   Default: varies
   Use: Distortion amount
   - -100%: Clean
   - 0%: Neutral
   - +100%: Maximum distortion

8. FILTER MODE
   Range: 0-3
   Default: varies
   Use: Filter mode selection
   - 0: Serial
   - 1: Parallel
   - 2: Band-pass
   - 3: Notch

9. SUB MIX
   Range: 0-100%
   Default: varies
   Use: Sub oscillator level
   - 0%: No sub
   - 50%: Balanced sub
   - 100%: Strong sub
