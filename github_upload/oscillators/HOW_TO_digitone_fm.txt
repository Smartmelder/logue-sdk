================================================================================
DIGITONE FM - 4-OPERATOR FM SYNTHESIZER
================================================================================

FEATURES:
- 4-operator FM synthesis
- 8 classic FM algorithms
- Per-operator envelopes
- Operator 1 feedback
- Frequency ratios (0.5× to 16×)
- LFO modulation
- Resonant filter
- Analog-style overdrive
- Built-in sequencer
- Step edit mode

================================================================================
HARDWARE CONTROLS
================================================================================

TYPE KNOB (Knob A):
  → Controls ALGORITHM (0-7)
  → Selects FM algorithm

SHAPE KNOB (Knob B):
  → Controls FM AMOUNT
  → Higher = more FM

================================================================================
10 PARAMETERS
================================================================================

0. ALGORITHM (TYPE knob)
   Range: 0-7
   Default: varies
   Use: FM algorithm selection
   - 0: Serial cascade (brass/bell)
   - 1: Parallel carriers (pad)
   - 2: Dual stacks (organ)
   - 3: Mixed (electric piano)
   - 4: One modulator (bass)
   - 5: Asymmetric (lead)
   - 6: All parallel (additive)
   - 7: Triple mod (complex)

1. FM AMOUNT (SHAPE knob)
   Range: 0-100%
   Default: varies
   Use: FM modulation amount
   - 0%: No FM
   - 50%: Moderate FM
   - 100%: Maximum FM

2. RATIO
   Range: 0-100%
   Default: varies
   Use: Frequency ratio
   - 0%: Low ratio
   - 50%: Moderate ratio
   - 100%: High ratio

3. FEEDBACK
   Range: 0-100%
   Default: varies
   Use: Operator 1 feedback
   - 0%: No feedback
   - 50%: Moderate feedback
   - 100%: Maximum feedback

4. ATTACK
   Range: 0-100%
   Default: varies
   Use: Attack time
   - 0%: Quick attack
   - 50%: Moderate attack
   - 100%: Slow attack

5. DECAY
   Range: 0-100%
   Default: varies
   Use: Decay time
   - 0%: Quick decay
   - 50%: Moderate decay
   - 100%: Slow decay

6. FILTER
   Range: 0-100%
   Default: varies
   Use: Filter cutoff
   - 0%: Closed
   - 50%: Mid
   - 100%: Open

7. RESONANCE
   Range: 0-100%
   Default: varies
   Use: Filter resonance
   - 0%: No resonance
   - 50%: Moderate resonance
   - 100%: Maximum resonance

8. PLAY
   Range: 0-1 (OFF/ON)
   Default: varies
   Use: Play control
   - 0: OFF
   - 1: ON

9. STEP EDIT
   Range: 0-15
   Default: varies
   Use: Step edit mode
   - 0-15: Different step positions
