================================================================================
PSY FLUTE - PSYCHEDELIC SYNTH-FLUTE OSCILLATOR
================================================================================

FEATURES:
- Flute-like core with dominant fundamental
- Breathy attack with noise burst
- Soft upper harmonics (not harsh)
- Expressive vibrato and evolving timbre
- Morphs from clean → house lead → psychedelic texture
- 3 flute types (Soft Wood, Bright Synth, Overblown Psy)
- Detune/spread for width
- Attack shaping (quick tongue → slow swell)
- Harmonic control (fundamental vs upper partials)
- Space helper (pre-emphasis for reverb/delay)

================================================================================
HARDWARE CONTROLS
================================================================================

TYPE KNOB (Knob A):
  → Controls FLUTE TYPE
  → 0-33%: Soft Wood, 33-66%: Bright Synth, 66-100%: Overblown Psy

SHAPE KNOB (Knob B):
  → Controls BREATH
  → Higher = more breath noise

================================================================================
10 PARAMETERS
================================================================================

0. FLUTE TYPE (TYPE knob)
   Range: 0-100%
   Default: 40% (Bright Synth)
   Use: Flute character selection
   - 0-33%: Soft Wood (mellow, low harmonics)
   - 33-66%: Bright Synth (forward mids)
   - 66-100%: Overblown Psy (strong upper harmonics)

1. BREATH (SHAPE knob)
   Range: 0-100%
   Default: 30%
   Use: Band-passed noise amount
   - 0%: No breath
   - 50%: Moderate breath
   - 100%: Maximum breath

2. BRIGHTNESS
   Range: 0-100%
   Default: 50%
   Use: Internal tone filter
   - 0%: Dark
   - 50%: Neutral
   - 100%: Bright

3. VIB RATE
   Range: 0-100%
   Default: 40%
   Use: Vibrato speed (0-10 Hz)
   - 0%: Very slow (0.5 Hz)
   - 50%: Moderate (5 Hz)
   - 100%: Fast (10 Hz)

4. VIB DEPTH
   Range: 0-100%
   Default: 30%
   Use: Vibrato amount
   - 0%: No vibrato
   - 50%: Moderate vibrato
   - 100%: Maximum vibrato

5. MOTION
   Range: 0-100%
   Default: 20%
   Use: Evolving timbre (brightness + breath)
   - 0%: No motion
   - 50%: Moderate motion
   - 100%: Maximum motion

6. SPREAD
   Range: 0-100%
   Default: 30%
   Use: Detune/phase for width
   - 0%: No spread
   - 50%: Moderate spread
   - 100%: Maximum spread

7. ATTACK
   Range: 0-100%
   Default: 50%
   Use: Attack shape
   - 0%: Quick tongue
   - 50%: Moderate attack
   - 100%: Slow swell

8. HARM TILT
   Range: 0-100%
   Default: 50%
   Use: Fundamental vs upper partials
   - 0%: More fundamental
   - 50%: Balanced
   - 100%: More harmonics

9. SPACE
   Range: 0-100%
   Default: 50%
   Use: Pre-emphasis for reverb/delay
   - 0%: Dry (upfront)
   - 50%: Moderate
   - 100%: Optimized for deep FX
