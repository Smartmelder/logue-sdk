================================================================================
ADVSEQ - ADVANCED SEQUENCER
================================================================================

FEATURES:
- Clock mode (internal/external)
- Sequence length (1-128 steps)
- Slice length (1-32 steps)
- Smoothing control
- Modulation depth
- 8 operations
- Shift (-64 to +64)
- Rate division (0-4)
- Swing control
- Dry/wet mix

================================================================================
HARDWARE CONTROLS
================================================================================

TYPE KNOB (Knob A):
  → Controls CLOCK MODE (0-1)
  → Internal/External

SHAPE KNOB (Knob B):
  → Controls SEQ LENGTH (1-128)
  → Sequence length

================================================================================
10 PARAMETERS
================================================================================

0. CLOCK MODE (TYPE knob)
   Range: 0-1
   Default: varies
   Use: Clock source
   - 0: Internal
   - 1: External

1. SEQ LENGTH (SHAPE knob)
   Range: 1-128
   Default: varies
   Use: Sequence length
   - 1: 1 step
   - 64: 64 steps
   - 128: 128 steps

2. SLICE LENGTH
   Range: 1-32
   Default: varies
   Use: Slice length
   - 1: 1 step
   - 16: 16 steps
   - 32: 32 steps

3. SMOOTHING
   Range: 0-100%
   Default: varies
   Use: Smoothing amount
   - 0%: No smoothing
   - 50%: Moderate smoothing
   - 100%: Maximum smoothing

4. MOD DEPTH
   Range: 0-100%
   Default: varies
   Use: Modulation depth
   - 0%: No modulation
   - 50%: Moderate modulation
   - 100%: Maximum modulation

5. OPERATION
   Range: 0-7
   Default: varies
   Use: Operation mode
   - 0-7: Different operations

6. SHIFT
   Range: -64 to +64
   Default: varies
   Use: Pitch shift
   - -64: Down
   - 0: No shift
   - +64: Up

7. RATE DIV
   Range: 0-4
   Default: varies
   Use: Rate division
   - 0-4: Different divisions

8. SWING
   Range: 0-100%
   Default: varies
   Use: Swing amount
   - 0%: Straight timing
   - 50%: Moderate swing
   - 100%: Maximum swing

9. MIX
   Range: 0-100%
   Default: varies
   Use: Dry/wet balance
   - 0%: Full dry
   - 50%: Balanced
   - 100%: Full wet
