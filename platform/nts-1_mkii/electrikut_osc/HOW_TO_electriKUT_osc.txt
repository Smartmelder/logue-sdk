================================================================================
ELECTRIKUT OSC 
================================================================================

FEATURES:
- 3 ElectriKUT modes (VA / PCM / FX)
- Digital grit (bit crushing + distortion)
- Mid-focused body control
- Transient snap shaper
- Shape motion (stepping feel)
- Voice stacking (density)


================================================================================
HARDWARE CONTROLS
================================================================================

TYPE KNOB (Knob A):
  → Controls E-MODE (ElectriKUT mode)
  → 0-33%: VA MODE (Classic saw/square)
  → 34-66%: PCM MODE (Attacky, sampler-like)
  → 67-100%: FX MODE (Metallic/formant)

SHAPE KNOB (Knob B):
  → Controls WAVE CHAR (Wave character)
  → Higher = more metallic/FM-like

================================================================================
10 PARAMETERS
================================================================================

0. E-MODE (TYPE knob)
   Range: 0-100%
   Default: 33% (VA MODE)
   Use: ElectriKUT mode selection
   - 0-33%: VA MODE (Classic saw/square with digital edge)
   - 34-66%: PCM MODE (Attacky, sampler-like stacks)
   - 67-100%: FX MODE (Metallic/formant for techno bleeps)

1. WAVE CHAR (SHAPE knob)
   Range: 0-100%
   Default: 50%
   Use: Wave character
   - 0%: Basic saw, punchy ElectriKUT basis
   - 50%: Shaped, formant-like
   - 100%: Metallic, synth-FX elektro sounds

2. GRIT
   Range: 0-100%
   Default: 30%
   Use: Digital edge/roughness
   - 0%: Cleaner (but still digital)
   - 50%: Moderate grit
   - 100%: Raw, aliasing, perfect for techno

3. BODY
   Range: 0-100%
   Default: 60%
   Use: Mid-focus amount
   - 0%: Thinner, more top-end
   - 50%: Balanced
   - 100%: Thick mid punch, "in your face"

4. SNAP
   Range: 0-100%
   Default: 50%
   Use: Transient sharpness
   - 0%: Rounder starts
   - 50%: Moderate snap
   - 100%: Sharp percussive click, rhythmic

5. MOTION
   Range: 0-100%
   Default: 20%
   Use: Internal shape movement
   - 0%: No motion
   - 50%: Moderate stepping feel
   - 100%: Strong robotic movement

6. SPREAD
   Range: 0-100%
   Default: 30%
   Use: Stereo width
   - 0%: Mono/narrow
   - 50%: Normal stereo
   - 100%: Wide (but still compact ElectriKUT-style)

7. DENSITY
   Range: 0-100%
   Default: 40%
   Use: Voice/layer count
   - 0%: Single voice, direct
   - 50%: Moderate stacking
   - 100%: Stacked, chord-ready

8. COLOR
   Range: 0-100%
   Default: 50%
   Use: Harmonic tilt
   - 0%: Darker, more fundamental
   - 50%: Balanced
   - 100%: Brighter, more upper harmonics

9. PUNCH
   Range: 0-100%
   Default: 40%
   Use: Attack enhancement
   - 0%: Normal envelope
   - 50%: Moderate punch
   - 100%: Extra click/punch for patterns
