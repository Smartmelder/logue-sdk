================================================================================
ETERNAL FLANGER - BARBER-POLE FLANGER EFFECT
================================================================================

FEATURES:
- Barber-pole illusion (endless rising/falling sweep)
- 3 direction modes (UP / DOWN / BOTH)
- 4× cascaded delay stages
- Smooth crossfading (no audible resets)
- Chorus → Classic → Extreme modes
- Stereo width control
- Feedback + resonance
- Tone shaping

================================================================================
HARDWARE CONTROLS
================================================================================

TYPE KNOB (Knob A):
  → Controls DIRECTION (sweep direction)
  → UP: Endless rising sweep
  → DOWN: Endless falling sweep
  → BOTH: Classic cycling up/down

SHAPE KNOB (Knob B):
  → Controls RATE (sweep speed)
  → Higher = faster sweep

================================================================================
10 PARAMETERS
================================================================================

0. DIRECTION (TYPE knob)
   Range: 0-2 (UP/DOWN/BOTH)
   Default: 2 (BOTH)
   Use: Sweep direction mode
   - 0: UP (endless rising sweep)
   - 1: DOWN (endless falling sweep)
   - 2: BOTH (classic cycling)

1. RATE (SHAPE knob)
   Range: 0-100%
   Default: 30%
   Use: Sweep speed
   - 0%: Very slow (0.05 Hz)
   - 50%: Moderate (4 Hz)
   - 100%: Fast (8 Hz)

2. DEPTH
   Range: 0-100%
   Default: 50%
   Use: Sweep intensity
   - 0%: Subtle chorus-like
   - 50%: Classic flanger
   - 100%: Extreme psychedelic

3. FEEDBACK
   Range: 0-100%
   Default: 40%
   Use: Resonance character
   - 0%: No feedback
   - 50%: Moderate resonance
   - 100%: Maximum feedback

4. MIX
   Range: 0-100%
   Default: 50%
   Use: Dry/wet balance
   - 0%: Full dry
   - 50%: Balanced
   - 100%: Full wet

5. STEREO
   Range: 0-100%
   Default: 60% (120% width)
   Use: Stereo width (0-200%)
   - 0%: Mono/narrow
   - 50%: Normal stereo (100%)
   - 100%: Ultra-wide (200%)

6. TONE
   Range: 0-100%
   Default: 50%
   Use: Brightness filter
   - 0%: Dark (low-pass)
   - 50%: Neutral
   - 100%: Bright (high-pass)

7. SMOOTH
   Range: 0-100%
   Default: 70%
   Use: Crossfade smoothness
   - 0%: Sharp transitions
   - 50%: Moderate smoothing
   - 100%: Maximum smoothness

8. STAGES
   Range: 2-4
   Default: 4
   Use: Number of delay stages
   - 2: Minimal (simpler)
   - 3: Moderate
   - 4: Maximum (full barber-pole)

9. RESONATE
   Range: 0-100%
   Default: 30%
   Use: Peak emphasis
   - 0%: No resonance boost
   - 50%: Moderate peak
   - 100%: Maximum resonance
