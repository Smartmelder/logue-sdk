================================================================================
SEQUENCE FILTER - TEMPO-SYNCED STEP SEQUENCER FILTER
================================================================================

FEATURES:
- 16-step pattern sequencer
- Tempo-synced filter modulation
- 3 cutoff ranges (HIGH, MID, LOW)
- Resonance control
- Pattern selection
- Feedback control
- 16 tempo divisions
- 8 preset patterns
- Sync on/off
- Forward/reverse direction

================================================================================
HARDWARE CONTROLS
================================================================================

TYPE KNOB (Knob A):
  → Controls RANGE (cutoff range)
  → HIGH / MID / LOW

SHAPE KNOB (Knob B):
  → Controls RESONANCE
  → Higher = more resonance

================================================================================
10 PARAMETERS
================================================================================

0. RANGE (TYPE knob)
   Range: 0-100%
   Default: 50% (MID)
   Use: Filter cutoff range
   - 0-33%: LOW (safe low range)
   - 33-66%: MID (medium range)
   - 66-100%: HIGH (safe high range)

1. RESONANCE (SHAPE knob)
   Range: 0-100%
   Default: 60%
   Use: Filter resonance amount
   - 0%: No resonance
   - 50%: Moderate resonance
   - 100%: Maximum resonance

2. SPEED
   Range: 0-100%
   Default: 75%
   Use: Pattern speed
   - 0%: Very slow
   - 50%: Moderate speed
   - 100%: Fast

3. PATTERN
   Range: 0-100%
   Default: 30%
   Use: Pattern selection
   - 0%: Pattern 0
   - 50%: Pattern 4
   - 100%: Pattern 7

4. MIX
   Range: 0-100%
   Default: 50%
   Use: Dry/wet balance
   - 0%: Full dry
   - 50%: Balanced
   - 100%: Full wet

5. FEEDBACK
   Range: 0-100%
   Default: 40%
   Use: Feedback amount
   - 0%: No feedback
   - 50%: Moderate feedback
   - 100%: Maximum feedback

6. DIVISION
   Range: 0-15
   Default: 3
   Use: Tempo division
   - 0-2: 1/4 (straight, dotted, triplet)
   - 3-5: 1/8 (straight, dotted, triplet)
   - 6-8: 1/16 (straight, dotted, triplet)
   - 9-11: 1/32 (straight, dotted, triplet)
   - 12-15: Other divisions

7. PRESET
   Range: 0-7
   Default: 0
   Use: Preset pattern
   - 0-7: 8 different patterns

8. SYNC
   Range: 0-1 (OFF/ON)
   Default: 1 (ON)
   Use: Tempo sync
   - 0: OFF (free running)
   - 1: ON (tempo synced)

9. DIRECTION
   Range: 0-1 (FORWARD/REVERSE)
   Default: 0 (FORWARD)
   Use: Pattern direction
   - 0: Forward
   - 1: Reverse
