================================================================================
HOUSE STACK - HOUSE CHORD STACK OSCILLATOR
================================================================================

FEATURES:
- 5 wave types
- Detune control
- Stereo spread
- 16 chord intervals
- Chord spread control
- Tone (tilt EQ)
- Attack shape
- Harmonic bend
- Glide (portamento)
- Modulation amount

================================================================================
HARDWARE CONTROLS
================================================================================

TYPE KNOB (Knob A):
  → Controls WAVE TYPE (0-4)
  → Selects waveform

SHAPE KNOB (Knob B):
  → Controls DETUNE
  → Higher = more detune

================================================================================
10 PARAMETERS
================================================================================

0. WAVE TYPE (TYPE knob)
   Range: 0-4
   Default: varies
   Use: Waveform selection
   - 0-4: Different waveforms

1. DETUNE (SHAPE knob)
   Range: 0-100%
   Default: varies
   Use: Detuning spread
   - 0%: No detune
   - 50%: Moderate detune
   - 100%: Maximum detune

2. STEREO SPREAD
   Range: 0-100%
   Default: varies
   Use: Stereo width
   - 0%: Mono
   - 50%: Normal stereo
   - 100%: Ultra-wide

3. CHORD INTERVAL
   Range: 0-15
   Default: varies
   Use: Chord interval selection
   - 0-15: Different intervals

4. CHORD SPREAD
   Range: 0-100%
   Default: varies
   Use: Chord spread amount
   - 0%: Tight
   - 50%: Moderate spread
   - 100%: Wide spread

5. TONE (TILT EQ)
   Range: 0-100%
   Default: varies
   Use: Tone tilt EQ
   - 0%: Dark
   - 50%: Neutral
   - 100%: Bright

6. ATTACK SHAPE
   Range: 0-100%
   Default: varies
   Use: Attack envelope shape
   - 0%: Quick attack
   - 50%: Moderate attack
   - 100%: Slow attack

7. HARMONIC BEND
   Range: 0-100%
   Default: varies
   Use: Harmonic bending amount
   - 0%: No bend
   - 50%: Moderate bend
   - 100%: Maximum bend

8. GLIDE
   Range: 0-100%
   Default: varies
   Use: Portamento time
   - 0%: No glide
   - 50%: Moderate glide
   - 100%: Slow glide

9. MOD AMOUNT
   Range: 0-100%
   Default: varies
   Use: Modulation amount
   - 0%: No modulation
   - 50%: Moderate modulation
   - 100%: Maximum modulation
