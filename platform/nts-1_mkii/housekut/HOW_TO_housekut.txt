================================================================================
HOUSEKUT 
================================================================================

FEATURES:
- 4 character modes (House Bells, Trance Leads, house Piano, Classic Club)
- Unison detune for thickness
- Brightness/tone control
- Motion LFO for evolving timbre
- Attack envelope shaping
- Glide/portamento
- Vibrato for musical expression
- Warmth control for body
- Flavor blend (90s to modern)
- Sustain control (lead vs pluck)

================================================================================
HARDWARE CONTROLS
================================================================================

TYPE KNOB (Knob A):
  → Controls CHARACTER (sound type)
  → 0: BELLS, 1: TRANCE, 2: PIANO, 3: CLASSIC

SHAPE KNOB (Knob B):
  → Controls DETUNE (unison width/thickness)
  → Higher = more detune, thicker sound

SHIFT + B:
  → Access additional parameters via menu

================================================================================
10 PARAMETERS
================================================================================

0. CHARACTER (TYPE knob)
   Range: 0-3
   Default: 1 (TRANCE)
   Use: Sound type selection
   - 0: BELLS - House Bells (FM-like bright bells, sparkly)
   - 1: TRANCE - Trance Leads (Supersaw-style, emotional, wide)
   - 2: PIANO - Nederhouse Piano (Warm, smooth, piano-like)
   - 3: CLASSIC - Classic Club Lead (90s dance synth, timeless)

1. DETUNE (SHAPE knob)
   Range: 0-100%
   Default: 50%
   Use: Unison width/thickness
   - 0%: No detune (single voice)
   - 50%: Moderate detune (thick, wide)
   - 100%: Maximum detune (very thick, wide)

2. BRIGHTNESS
   Range: 0-100%
   Default: 60%
   Use: Tone color/filter
   - 0%: Dark (muffled, low-pass)
   - 50%: Neutral (balanced)
   - 100%: Bright (crisp, high-pass)

3. MOTION
   Range: 0-100%
   Default: 30%
   Use: Shape LFO movement
   - 0%: No motion (static)
   - 30%: Subtle movement
   - 100%: Maximum motion (evolving timbre)

4. ATTACK
   Range: 0-100%
   Default: 25%
   Use: Note attack style
   - 0%: Quick attack (instant)
   - 25%: Moderate attack
   - 100%: Slow attack (swell)

5. GLIDE
   Range: 0-100%
   Default: 20%
   Use: Legato portamento
   - 0%: No glide (instant pitch change)
   - 20%: Subtle glide
   - 100%: Maximum glide (smooth pitch transitions)

6. VIBRATO
   Range: 0-100%
   Default: 30%
   Use: Musical pitch vibrato
   - 0%: No vibrato
   - 30%: Subtle vibrato
   - 100%: Maximum vibrato (wide pitch modulation)

7. WARMTH
   Range: 0-100%
   Default: 50%
   Use: Body/low-mid presence
   - 0%: Thin (no body)
   - 50%: Balanced
   - 100%: Warm (full body, low-mid emphasis)

8. FLAVOR
   Range: 0-100%
   Default: 33%
   Use: 90s to modern blend
   - 0%: Classic 90s (more pulse, vintage)
   - 33%: Balanced
   - 100%: Modern (more saw, contemporary)

9. SUSTAIN
   Range: 0-100%
   Default: 75%
   Use: Lead vs pluck behavior
   - 0%: Pluck (short, percussive)
   - 50%: Balanced
   - 100%: Lead (long sustain, melodic)
