================================================================================
KUTCHORUS - MULTI-MODE CHORUS EFFECT
================================================================================

FEATURES:
- 4 chorus types (Soft, Classic, Bright, Wide)
- 2-4 voice chorus engine
- LFO modulation (rate + depth)
- Stereo width control
- Tone filter (dark to bright)
- Motion/randomization
- Bass cut (pump-safe)
- Feedback control

================================================================================
HARDWARE CONTROLS
================================================================================

TYPE KNOB (Knob A, with MOD held):
  → Controls TYPE (chorus mode)
  → 0-25%: Soft, 25-50%: Classic, 50-75%: Bright, 75-100%: Wide

SHAPE KNOB (Knob B):
  → Controls RATE (LFO speed)
  → Higher = faster modulation

SHIFT + B (MIX):
  → Controls DRY/WET MIX
  → 0% = full dry, 50% = balanced, 100% = full wet

================================================================================
10 PARAMETERS
================================================================================

0. TYPE
   Range: 0-100%
   Default: 25% (Classic)
   Use: Chorus type selection
   - 0-25%: Soft (gentle, warm)
   - 25-50%: Classic (standard chorus)
   - 50-75%: Bright (forward, cutting)
   - 75-100%: Wide (stereo spread)

1. RATE (SHAPE knob)
   Range: 0-100%
   Default: 30%
   Use: LFO modulation speed
   - 0%: Very slow (0.1 Hz)
   - 50%: Moderate (2-3 Hz)
   - 100%: Fast (5-10 Hz)

2. DEPTH
   Range: 0-100%
   Default: 50%
   Use: Modulation depth
   - 0%: No modulation
   - 50%: Moderate depth
   - 100%: Maximum depth

3. MIX (SHIFT + B)
   Range: 0-100%
   Default: 50%
   Use: Dry/wet balance
   - 0%: Full dry
   - 50%: Balanced (50/50)
   - 100%: Full wet

4. WIDTH
   Range: 0-100%
   Default: 50%
   Use: Stereo width
   - 0%: Mono
   - 50%: Normal stereo
   - 100%: Ultra-wide

5. TONE
   Range: 0-100%
   Default: 50%
   Use: Tone filter
   - 0%: Dark (low-pass)
   - 50%: Neutral
   - 100%: Bright (high-pass)

6. MOTION
   Range: 0-100%
   Default: 20%
   Use: Randomization amount
   - 0%: No motion (static)
   - 50%: Moderate motion
   - 100%: Maximum motion

7. BASS CUT
   Range: 0-100%
   Default: 30%
   Use: High-pass filter (pump-safe)
   - 0%: No cut
   - 50%: Moderate cut
   - 100%: Strong cut

8. VOICES
   Range: 2-4
   Default: 3
   Use: Number of chorus voices
   - 2: Dual chorus
   - 3: Triple chorus
   - 4: Quad chorus

9. FEEDBACK
   Range: 0-100%
   Default: 10%
   Use: Feedback amount
   - 0%: No feedback
   - 50%: Moderate feedback
   - 100%: Maximum feedback
