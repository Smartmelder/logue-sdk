INSTALLATION:

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
CONTROLS (NTS-1 mkII)
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

FRONT PANEL:
- TYPE knob: Select patch/preset
- A knob (SHAPE): TIMBRE (Timbre/Intensity)
- B knob (ALT): DECAY (Decay Time)

PARAMETER MENU:
Hold OSC button + turn TYPE knob to select parameter
Adjust with A/B knobs

PARAMETERS:
0. TIMBRE - Timbre/Intensity per engine (Range: 0-1023, Default: 614)
1. DECAY - Decay Time (Range: 0-1023, Default: 307)
2. ATTACK - Attack Time (Range: 0-1023, Default: 512)
3. RELEASE - Release Time (Range: 0-1023, Default: 768)
4. BRIGHT - Brightness (Range: 0-1023, Default: 409)
5. PUNCH - Punch amount (Range: 0-1023, Default: 666)
6. DETUNE - Detune amount (Range: 0-1023, Default: 256)
7. DRIVE - Drive/distortion (Range: 0-1023, Default: 358)
8. ENGINE - Engine selection (Range: 0-3, Default: 0)
9. PRESET - Preset selection (Range: 0-7, Default: 0)

FEATURES
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

90s RAVE MULTI-ENGINE

The sound of early 90s Eurodance, Rave, and House!

4 SYNTHESIS ENGINES:

1. THE HOOVER (Roland Alpha Juno):
   - PWM sawtooth with fast pitch LFO
   - "Vacuum cleaner" sound
   - Used in: 2 Unlimited, Human Resource, L.A. Style
   - Dual pulse waves with different PWM
   - Pitch envelope (rises then falls)

2. FM DONK (Yamaha TX81Z):
   - 2-operator FM with fast decay
   - Metallic, percussive bass
   - Used in: Quadrophonia, Praga Khan, Cubic 22
   - High modulation index (8-20)
   - Exponential decay envelope

3. RAVE SAW (Supersaw technique):
   - Multiple detuned saws
   - Thick, aggressive sound
   - Used in: Stabs, leads, pads
   - 3 sawtooth oscillators
   - Heavy detuning (10-50 cents)

4. HOUSE ORGAN (Korg M1 "Organ 2"):
   - Mixed waveforms with attack click
   - House music signature sound
   - Used in: Robin S, Crystal Waters, Ce Ce Peniston
   - Sine + filtered square mix
   - Sharp attack transient

ADDITIONAL FEATURES:
- Velocity sensitive
- Band-limited (PolyBLEP)
- Drive/distortion
- Authentic 90s character
- 8 classic presets
