OVERVIEW
================================================================================

S-1 TWEAK is inspired by Roland SH-101 and AIRA Compact S-1, optimized for:
- Techno bass (SH-101 style mono synth)
- House chords (paraphonic polyphony)
- Ratcheted leads (sub-steps like S-1)
- Evolving pads (motion recording)
- Metallic FX (OSC Draw/Chop)
- Ambient soundscapes

FEATURES:
- SH-101 style VA core (saw, pulse, sub, noise)
- S-1 OSC Draw (waveform morphing)
- S-1 OSC Chop/Comb (harmonic slicing)
- 4 modes: Mono / Para-Poly / Unison / Chord
- Built-in ratcheting (sub-steps like S-1)
- Motion recording (evolving patterns)
- Probability/humanization
- House-friendly chord progressions
- Works seamlessly with NTS-1 ARP/SEQ

Based on Korg logue SDK oscillator template

================================================================================
10 PARAMETERS
================================================================================

0. WAVE MIX (TYPE knob)
   Range: 0-100%
   Default: 50%
   Use: Blend between saw and pulse
   - 0%: Pure sawtooth
   - 50%: Balanced mix
   - 100%: Pure pulse/square

1. DRAW SHAPE (SHAPE knob)
   Range: 0-100%
   Default: 0%
   Use: S-1 OSC Draw waveform morphing
   - 0%: Pure waveforms (no morphing)
   - 50%: Moderate morphing
   - 100%: Maximum morphing (complex shapes)

2. CHOP/COMB
   Range: 0-100%
   Default: 0% (Off)
   Use: S-1 OSC Chop/Comb (harmonic slicing)
   - 0%: No chopping
   - 50%: Moderate chopping
   - 100%: Maximum chopping (metallic)

3. MODE
   Range: 0-100%
   Default: 0% (MONO)
   Use: Select playback mode
   - 0-24%: MONO (legato)
   - 25-49%: POLY (4 voices)
   - 50-74%: UNISON (stacked)
   - 75-100%: CHORD (house progressions)

4. CHORD TYPE
   Range: 0-100%
   Default: 0% (Major)
   Use: Chord voicing (when MODE = CHORD)
   - 0%: Major
   - 25%: Minor
   - 50%: 7th
   - 75%: Sus4
   - 100%: Custom

5. DETUNE/SPREAD
   Range: 0-100%
   Default: 30%
   Use: Detuning between voices
   - 0%: No detune (tight)
   - 50%: Moderate spread
   - 100%: Maximum spread (wide)

6. NOISE/RISER
   Range: 0-100%
   Default: 10%
   Use: White noise level
   - 0%: No noise
   - 50%: Moderate noise
   - 100%: Strong noise (texture)

7. RATTLE/SUBSTEP
   Range: 0-100%
   Default: 0% (Off)
   Use: Ratcheting (sub-steps like S-1)
   - 0%: No ratcheting
   - 50%: Moderate ratcheting
   - 100%: Maximum ratcheting (4Ã— repeats)

8. PROBABILITY
   Range: 0-100%
   Default: 0% (Deterministic)
   Use: Humanization/randomness
   - 0%: All notes play (deterministic)
   - 50%: 50% probability
   - 100%: Maximum randomness

9. MOTION
   Range: 0-100%
   Default: 0% (Static)
   Use: Motion recording (evolving patterns)
   - 0%: Static (no motion)
   - 50%: Moderate motion
   - 100%: Maximum motion (evolving)

================================================================================
TROUBLESHOOTING
================================================================================

Q: No sound output
A: Check MODE is set correctly, ensure WAVEMIX > 0%

Q: Sound too thin
A: Increase DETUNE (50-80%), use UNISON mode (50-74%)

Q: Ratcheting not working
A: Ensure RATTLE > 0%, works best with ARP/SEQ active

Q: Motion not audible
A: Increase MOTION (40-100%), works over time (evolving)

Q: Chord mode not working
A: Set MODE to 75-100% (CHORD), adjust CHORD TYPE
