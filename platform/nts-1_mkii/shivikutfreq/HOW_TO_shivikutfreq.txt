================================================================================
SHIVIKUTFREQ - FREQUENCY SHIFTING DUB DELAY
================================================================================

FEATURES:
- Real frequency shifter using Hilbert transform
- Linear Hz shift (not pitch shift!)
- Each repeat shifts in frequency
- Spiraling, gliding, sci-fi echoes
- Perfect for dub techno/house
- Tempo sync support
- Stereo width control
- Lo-fi/dirt processing
- Wander modulation

================================================================================
HARDWARE CONTROLS
================================================================================

TYPE KNOB (Knob A):
  → Controls TIME (delay length)
  → Higher = longer delays

SHAPE KNOB (Knob B):
  → Controls FEEDBACK (repeat amount)
  → Higher = more repeats

SHIFT + B (MIX):
  → Controls DRY/WET MIX
  → -100% = full dry, 0% = balanced, +100% = full wet

================================================================================
10 PARAMETERS
================================================================================

0. TIME (TYPE knob)
   Range: 0-100%
   Default: 50%
   Use: Delay time in seconds (or tempo-synced)
   - 0%: Short delay (0.01s)
   - 50%: Medium delay (0.5s)
   - 100%: Long delay (3.0s)
   Note: When SYNC is enabled, this becomes tempo-synced

1. FEEDBACK (SHAPE knob)
   Range: 0-100%
   Default: 60%
   Use: Feedback amount (repeat intensity)
   - 0%: No repeats (single echo)
   - 50%: Moderate repeats
   - 100%: Maximum feedback (long trails)

2. MIX (SHIFT + B)
   Range: -100% to +100%
   Default: 0% (Balanced)
   Use: Dry/wet balance
   - -100%: Full dry (no effect)
   - 0%: Balanced (50/50)
   - +100%: Full wet (effect only)

3. SHIFT HZ
   Range: 0-100%
   Default: 30%
   Use: Frequency shift amount in Hz
   - 0%: No shift (0 Hz)
   - 30%: Moderate shift (~5 Hz)
   - 100%: Maximum shift (~20 Hz)
   Note: This is LINEAR Hz shift, not pitch shift!

4. DIRECTION
   Range: 0-2 (OFF/UP/DOWN)
   Default: 1 (UP)
   Use: Frequency shift direction
   - 0: OFF (no frequency shift, normal delay)
   - 1: UP (shift frequencies up)
   - 2: DOWN (shift frequencies down)

5. TONE
   Range: 0-100%
   Default: 40%
   Use: Tone filter (dark to bright)
   - 0%: Dark (low-pass, muffled)
   - 50%: Neutral (balanced)
   - 100%: Bright (high-pass, crisp)

6. STEREO
   Range: 0-100%
   Default: 75%
   Use: Stereo width control
   - 0%: Mono
   - 50%: Normal stereo
   - 100%: Ultra-wide stereo spread

7. WANDER
   Range: 0-100%
   Default: 25%
   Use: Modulation/movement amount
   - 0%: No modulation (static)
   - 25%: Subtle movement
   - 100%: Maximum modulation (chaotic)

8. SYNC
   Range: 0-8
   Default: 3
   Use: Tempo sync divisions
   - 0: OFF (free time)
   - 1: 1/16 note
   - 2: 1/8 note
   - 3: 3/16 note
   - 4: 1/4 note
   - 5: 3/8 note
   - 6: 1/2 note
   - 7: 3/4 note
   - 8: 1/1 note (whole note)

9. LOFI
   Range: 0-100%
   Default: 20%
   Use: Lo-fi/dirt amount
   - 0%: Clean (no degradation)
   - 20%: Subtle lo-fi character
   - 100%: Maximum dirt (crushed, degraded)
