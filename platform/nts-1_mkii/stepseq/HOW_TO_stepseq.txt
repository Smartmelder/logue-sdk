================================================================================
STEPSEQ - STEP SEQUENCER MODULATION
================================================================================

FEATURES:
- Play control
- 16-step selection
- Pitch offset (-24 to +24 semitones)
- Filter modulation
- Gate length control
- Sequence length (1-16 steps)
- Swing amount
- Ratchet (0-3)
- 8 patterns
- 4 directions

================================================================================
HARDWARE CONTROLS
================================================================================

TYPE KNOB (Knob A):
  → Controls PLAY (0-1)
  → ON/OFF

SHAPE KNOB (Knob B):
  → Controls STEP SELECT (0-15)
  → Selects step

================================================================================
10 PARAMETERS
================================================================================

0. PLAY (TYPE knob)
   Range: 0-1 (OFF/ON)
   Default: varies
   Use: Play control
   - 0: OFF
   - 1: ON

1. STEP SELECT (SHAPE knob)
   Range: 0-15
   Default: varies
   Use: Step selection
   - 0-15: Different steps

2. PITCH OFFSET
   Range: -24 to +24 semitones
   Default: varies
   Use: Pitch offset per step
   - -24: Two octaves down
   - 0: No offset
   - +24: Two octaves up

3. FILTER MOD
   Range: 0-100%
   Default: varies
   Use: Filter modulation amount
   - 0%: No modulation
   - 50%: Moderate modulation
   - 100%: Maximum modulation

4. GATE LENGTH
   Range: 0-100%
   Default: varies
   Use: Gate time
   - 0%: Very short
   - 50%: Moderate gate
   - 100%: Full gate

5. SEQ LENGTH
   Range: 1-16
   Default: varies
   Use: Sequence length
   - 1: 1 step
   - 8: 8 steps
   - 16: 16 steps

6. SWING AMOUNT
   Range: 0-100%
   Default: varies
   Use: Swing/shuffle amount
   - 0%: Straight timing
   - 50%: Moderate swing
   - 100%: Maximum swing

7. RATCHET
   Range: 0-3
   Default: varies
   Use: Ratchet amount
   - 0: No ratchet
   - 1: Single ratchet
   - 2: Double ratchet
   - 3: Triple ratchet

8. PATTERN
   Range: 0-7
   Default: varies
   Use: Pattern selection
   - 0-7: Different patterns

9. DIRECTION
   Range: 0-3
   Default: varies
   Use: Playback direction
   - 0: Forward
   - 1: Reverse
   - 2: Ping-pong
   - 3: Random
