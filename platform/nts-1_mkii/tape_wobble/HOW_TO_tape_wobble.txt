================================================================================
TAPE WOBBLE - VINTAGE TAPE MACHINE SIMULATOR
================================================================================

FEATURES:
- Wow (slow pitch variation, 0.1-2 Hz)
- Flutter (fast pitch variation, 5-20 Hz)
- Saturation (tape saturation)
- Noise (tape hiss)
- Compression (tape compression)
- Warble (pitch modulation)
- Age (degradation)
- 8 tape types
- 4 playback speeds
- Dry/wet mix

================================================================================
HARDWARE CONTROLS
================================================================================

TYPE KNOB (Knob A):
  → Controls TAPE TYPE (0-7)
  → Selects tape character

SHAPE KNOB (Knob B):
  → Controls WOW (wow amount)
  → Higher = more wow

SHIFT + B:
  → Controls FLUTTER (flutter amount)
  → Higher = more flutter

================================================================================
10 PARAMETERS
================================================================================

0. WOW (SHAPE knob)
   Range: 0-100%
   Default: 60%
   Use: Slow pitch variation (0.1-2 Hz)
   - 0%: No wow
   - 50%: Moderate wow
   - 100%: Maximum wow

1. FLUTTER (SHIFT + B)
   Range: 0-100%
   Default: 50%
   Use: Fast pitch variation (5-20 Hz)
   - 0%: No flutter
   - 50%: Moderate flutter
   - 100%: Maximum flutter

2. SATURATION
   Range: 0-100%
   Default: 75%
   Use: Tape saturation amount
   - 0%: Clean
   - 50%: Moderate saturation
   - 100%: Maximum saturation

3. NOISE
   Range: 0-100%
   Default: 30%
   Use: Tape hiss level
   - 0%: No noise
   - 50%: Moderate noise
   - 100%: Maximum noise

4. COMPRESS
   Range: 0-100%
   Default: 40%
   Use: Tape compression amount
   - 0%: No compression
   - 50%: Moderate compression
   - 100%: Maximum compression

5. WARBLE
   Range: 0-100%
   Default: 25%
   Use: Pitch modulation amount
   - 0%: No warble
   - 50%: Moderate warble
   - 100%: Maximum warble

6. AGE
   Range: 0-100%
   Default: 65%
   Use: Tape degradation/age
   - 0%: New tape
   - 50%: Moderate age
   - 100%: Old/worn tape

7. MIX
   Range: 0-100%
   Default: 50%
   Use: Dry/wet balance
   - 0%: Full dry
   - 50%: Balanced
   - 100%: Full wet

8. TAPE TYPE (TYPE knob)
   Range: 0-7
   Default: 2
   Use: Tape character selection
   - 0-7: Different tape types

9. SPEED
   Range: 0-3
   Default: 1
   Use: Playback speed
   - 0: Slow
   - 1: Normal
   - 2: Fast
   - 3: Very fast
