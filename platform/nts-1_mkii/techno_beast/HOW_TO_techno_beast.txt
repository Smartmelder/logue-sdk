================================================================================
TECHNO BEAST - ULTIMATE LEAD/BASS OSCILLATOR
================================================================================

FEATURES:
- Supersaw/Supersquare unison engine (3-7 voices)
- Octave doubling mode (perfect octaves)
- Sub oscillator (-1 & -2 octaves)
- Hard sync (classic acid techno)
- PWM modulation (auto-sweep)
- Built-in resonant filter
- Analog-style overdrive
- Velocity sensitivity (accent)
- Portamento (glide)
- Phase spread control

================================================================================
HARDWARE CONTROLS
================================================================================

TYPE KNOB (Knob A):
  → Selects MODE (0-3)
  → UNISON SAW / UNISON SQR / OCTAVE SAW / OCTAVE SQR

SHAPE KNOB (Knob B):
  → Controls DETUNE/SPREAD
  → Higher = more detuning between voices

================================================================================
11 PARAMETERS
================================================================================

0. MODE (TYPE knob)
   Range: 0-3
   Default: 0 (UNISON SAW)
   Use: Select oscillator mode
   - 0: UNISON SAW (7 voices, classic supersaw)
   - 1: UNISON SQR (5 voices, supersquare)
   - 2: OCTAVE SAW (3 octaves, sawtooth)
   - 3: OCTAVE SQR (3 octaves, square)

1. DETUNE (SHAPE knob)
   Range: 0-100%
   Default: 60%
   Use: Detuning spread between unison voices
   - 0%: No detune (tight, focused)
   - 50%: Moderate spread (musical)
   - 100%: Maximum spread (wide, lush)

2. SUB MIX
   Range: 0-100%
   Default: 40%
   Use: Sub oscillator level (-1 & -2 octaves)
   - 0%: No sub
   - 50%: Balanced sub
   - 100%: Strong sub (thick bass)

3. SYNC
   Range: 0-100%
   Default: 0% (Off)
   Use: Hard sync amount (classic acid techno)
   - 0%: No sync
   - 50%: Moderate sync (harmonic)
   - 100%: Maximum sync (aggressive)

4. PWM
   Range: 0-100%
   Default: 30%
   Use: Pulse width modulation depth
   - 0%: No PWM
   - 50%: Moderate PWM (musical)
   - 100%: Maximum PWM (expressive)

5. FILTER
   Range: 0-100%
   Default: 80%
   Use: Filter cutoff frequency
   - 0%: Closed (dark)
   - 50%: Mid (balanced)
   - 100%: Open (bright)

6. RESONANCE
   Range: 0-100%
   Default: 30%
   Use: Filter resonance amount
   - 0%: No resonance
   - 50%: Moderate resonance
   - 100%: Maximum resonance (self-oscillation)

7. DRIVE
   Range: 0-100%
   Default: 20%
   Use: Overdrive/distortion amount
   - 0%: Clean
   - 50%: Moderate drive
   - 100%: Maximum drive (distorted)

8. ACCENT
   Range: 0-100%
   Default: 50%
   Use: Velocity sensitivity
   - 0%: No velocity response
   - 50%: Moderate accent
   - 100%: Maximum velocity sensitivity

9. GLIDE
   Range: 0-100%
   Default: 0% (Off)
   Use: Portamento time
   - 0%: No glide (instant)
   - 50%: Moderate glide
   - 100%: Slow glide (smooth)

10. PHASE SPREAD
    Range: 0-100%
    Default: 0% (Off)
    Use: Phase randomization between voices
    - 0%: No spread (synchronized)
    - 50%: Moderate spread
    - 100%: Maximum spread (random phases)
