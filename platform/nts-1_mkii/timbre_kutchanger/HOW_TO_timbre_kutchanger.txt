================================================================================
TIMBRE-KUTCHANGER - ULTIMATE TIMBRE MORPHING MODFX
================================================================================

FEATURES:
- Transform any oscillator into different characters
- 5 character modes (Electric, Metallic, Flute, Alt/Mezzo, Soprano)
- Formant filtering for vocal/brass-like tones
- Harmonic emphasis control
- Ensemble/chorus effect for width
- Vibrato/motion for expression
- Attack shaping for transients
- Smooth character morphing (0-100%)

================================================================================
HARDWARE CONTROLS
================================================================================

TYPE KNOB (Knob A):
  → Controls CHARACTER (0-100%)
  → 0-19%: Electric, 20-39%: Metallic, 40-59%: Flute, 60-79%: Alt/Mezzo, 80-100%: Soprano

SHAPE KNOB (Knob B):
  → Controls BRIGHTNESS (tone color)
  → Higher = brighter, more edge

SHIFT + B:
  → Access additional parameters via menu

================================================================================
10 PARAMETERS
================================================================================

0. CHARACTER (TYPE knob)
   Range: 0-100%
   Default: 50%
   Use: Main timbre selector
   - 0-19%: ELECTRIC - Bright, synth-achtig, dance/house leads
   - 20-39%: METALLIC - High resonances, techno/psy vibes, comb-like
   - 40-59%: FLUTE - Soft, breathy, melodic, acoustic feel
   - 60-79%: ALT/MEZZO - Warm, vocal body, mid-range presence
   - 80-100%: SOPRANO - Bright, brilliant, high register, expressive

1. BRIGHTNESS (SHAPE knob)
   Range: 0-100%
   Default: 50%
   Use: Tone color/edge/helderheid
   - 0%: Dark (muffled, low-pass)
   - 50%: Neutral (balanced)
   - 100%: Bright (crisp, high-pass, cutting)

2. FORMANT
   Range: 0-100%
   Default: 40%
   Use: Vocal/brass body (formant frequency)
   - 0%: Low formants (warm, body)
   - 50%: Mid formants (balanced)
   - 100%: High formants (bright, forward)

3. MOTION
   Range: 0-100%
   Default: 30%
   Use: Vibrato/expressie speed
   - 0%: No motion (static)
   - 30%: Subtle vibrato
   - 100%: Fast vibrato (expressive)

4. ENSEMBLE
   Range: 0-100%
   Default: 50%
   Use: Stereo width/chorus effect
   - 0%: Mono (no width)
   - 50%: Normal stereo width
   - 100%: Maximum width (wide, chorus-like)

5. HARMONIC
   Range: 0-100%
   Default: 50%
   Use: Harmonic emphasis (tilt EQ)
   - 0%: Low harmonics (warm, body)
   - 50%: Balanced harmonics
   - 100%: High harmonics (bright, cutting)

6. ATTACK
   Range: 0-100%
   Default: 50%
   Use: Attack response shaping
   - 0%: Fast attack (instant, percussive)
   - 50%: Moderate attack
   - 100%: Slow attack (swell, smooth)

7. MIX
   Range: 0-100%
   Default: 75%
   Use: Dry/wet blend
   - 0%: Full dry (no effect)
   - 50%: Balanced (50/50)
   - 100%: Full wet (effect only)

8. COLOR
   Range: 0-100%
   Default: 50%
   Use: Color variation within character
   - 0%: Darker variant
   - 50%: Standard color
   - 100%: Brighter variant

9. DEPTH
   Range: 0-100%
   Default: 60%
   Use: Effect intensity
   - 0%: Subtle effect
   - 60%: Moderate intensity
   - 100%: Maximum intensity (strong transformation)
